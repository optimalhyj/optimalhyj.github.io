Research Interests

Working at the phonetics–phonology interface: how articulation shapes phonological representation, how speech is planned, and how it comes to carry social, affective, and clinical meaning.

  • Articulatory basis of phonological representation
  • Experimental phonetics of speech production & planning
  • Sound symbolism, iconicity & affective phonology
  • Register & sociophonetics — Korean Aegyo, hypocoristics
  • Speech, clinical phonetics & language AI
